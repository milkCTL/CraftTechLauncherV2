English:
Please download the toolchain first, such as the MingGW compiler and NASM assembler, and unzip it into the Tools folder in your project.
The link is as follows MingGW:https://winlibs.com/ NASM:https://www.nasm.us/pub/nasm/releasebuilds/3.01/win64/
It is recommended to download the Win64 and .7z versions.
It is recommended to download the Win64 ZIP version of NASM and unzip it to the Tools directory.
简体中文
请先下载工具链（例如 MingGW 编译器和 NASM 汇编器），并将其解压到项目中的 Tools 文件夹内。

MingGW：https://winlibs.com/
NASM：https://www.nasm.us/pub/nasm/releasebuilds/3.01/win64/
建议下载 Win64 位的.7z 格式版本。
建议下载 NASM 的 Win64 位 ZIP 版本，并将其解压至 Tools 目录。
